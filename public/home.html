About Me

I am from Davis, California and lived in Santa Barbara and San Jose for colleges. I have an older sister, step-brother and a 1 year old dog, Atlas. As more of an independent person, I enjoy food, music, exercising, traveling, sports, and learning everyday. Summer of 2017, after a road-trip to Kentucky while visiting friends with a buddy of mine, is when I was inspired to finally commit my life to computer programming and web development. I have always carried a nerdy side to me. I have always craved the latest tech, beginning with Gameboy's and Gamecube's from my childhood. None of my schools taught any CS growing up. And true enough, web development was never my hearts passion throughout my early years of adulthood. One day, I would love to work in the blockchain industry to help expand its potential everyday uses. I'm 26, today is the first day of the rest of my life. The journey feels perfect because I find purpose through programming.
